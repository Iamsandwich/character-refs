Vokunaar

Vokunaar is based in the Elder Scrolls universe, inspired specifically by the Morrowind daedra.

He was summoned by a tall elf Vermis, who simply wanted some help in starting a fire and cooking a meal. But Vakunaar is a far stronger being, and yet he obeyed as a summoned being, and did what he was told without being rude.

Later he claimed the throne as a daedric prince, but he isn't yet sure what exactly he is going to govern over, so for now his home is all his, and Vermis has been dragged into his business as well.

Vermis has pitch-black or black-blue skin with big shiny red eyes with yellow pupils. His eyebrows are a glowing pattern, as if lava flowing beneath his skin.

He's got pointy ears and short curved golden horns with a smooth surface, and he dresses fancily. A good reference is the daedra of Morrowind.

His soulmate and summoner is Vermis.
