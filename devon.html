Devon

Devon is a genetically modified human. He was made specifically to be a very productive programmer and developer for complex space stations, space ships and robotic equipment. He can build robots for any needed task and optimises and debugs code as his main job.

As a special unit, he is made to be very effective and attentive, at the cost of needing way more calories than his body would otherwise. His diet is mainly sugary treats and drinks due to that, without them he'll get very slow and lethargic. He likes tasty fruit sodas.

His hair has a pale colorful streak that is a natural side-effect. He loves his work, but can get carried off, so he has a special watch he mustn't take off, issued by the company, that monitors his health emotionally and physically.

He is physically not strong at all, so his robots do all the jobs for him. Iver is ab old model of a genetically altered human warrior. Made for harsh enviroments and large-scale battles, he is unstable in the same way Devon is. Together they keep one-another in check.

In an alternate scenario Iver is a robot built by Devon as an experiment on human conciousness.

His partner and caretaker is Iver.
