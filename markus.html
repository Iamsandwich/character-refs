Markus

Markus is a character made by my SO, but I was allowed to take him, and change him to my liking (at least my version). In the original design he has spiky red hair, and a leather jacket.
Markus is a vampire who lives in a strange world, where night reigns forever. He dislikes the ruling power, which wants to pick him as a new ruler and wishes to overthrow gods and make sun rule the world again!
He takes in a young not yet converted man from a good family, experiments to turn him into a strong being, and later feeds his blood to him to slowly turn him into a vampire who fears no sun.
He is very fancy and exists in many time perionds, so he is fashionable in many outfits of many time periods.

In an alternate scenario Alistair is a vampire hunter and Markus lives a secluded life in his castle near a village.

His beloved and experiment is Alistair.
