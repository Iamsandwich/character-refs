Raclette

Raclette is a shadow being who almost never changes his shape. He lives in the sewers in a world below the surface, where resources are scarse and a separate society of creatures lives with their own laws.

Raclette has been a ruler of his territory for a few years, despite his youth. He criticised his society for praising a cult of a Crocodile-like being named "Emperor" who supposedly used to rule the land before everyone had to retreat to live underground.

One day Emperor awoke from his long sleep, and Raclette could no longer be the solitary ruler of his land. Very slowly, with little trust, Raclette warmed up to him, and now he is a crowned Empress of the kingdom.

Raclette looks like a human with fuzzy rat ears and a long rat tail. He paints his face to look menacing by applying white paint in the shape of a skull, and dyes his hazel hair a bright fuxia.

He likes fancy jewelry and nice clothes, his favourite snack is orange soda, and he likes human videogames.

His beloved is Emperor.
