Sammy

Sammy, or Samuel is an interdimentional being who is also your local mob boss.
He is young by the standards of his species, and is the future emperor of all dimensions.
He is silly, impulsive, and suffers from varous mental problems due to living in the 3rd dimension.
He can "go through walls" by merging into dimensons. There is always only one him across all plains of existence.
His official title is "The third Dimension Lord, the most accurate projection of the Dimension Emperor".
He has two brothers, one of which exiled him in his childhood, knows maths and physics better than any human,
loves his mob family and soda.
His doctor is Michael, who works for him in the mob.

He was named by Mr. T. Bone, who is a t-bone objecthead, who took him in at an early age. His culture has no names. Usually people call him Sanwich or just Boss. But Michael always calls him Samuel.

He has an objecthead design, where his head is a long black needle that can move around the axis. He disguises himself by putting food onto it, usually decoy sandwiches. It's a parallel - sandwich is his disguise as much as color lenses help him blend in with humans, as his more human form doesn't have them visible.

His partner and bodyguard is Ains.

Main traits:
Blonde hair
Blank irises (uses lenses of any color, usually blue or green)
Objecthead - sandwich (actually a needle inserted in)
Likes bright colors
Can't recognise faces
Gun.

Age:
Visually 25-27, in reality thousands of years old

Sammy exists in the same universe as Michael, Ulven, John and partially, Adam.
