Sammy

Sammy, or Samuel is an interdimentional being who is also your local mob boss.
He is young by the standards of his species, and is the future emperor of all dimensions.
He is silly, impulsive, and suffers from varous mental problems due to living in the 3rd dimension.
He can "go through walls" by merging into dimensons. There is always only one him across all plains of existence.
His official title is "The third Dimension Lord, the most accurate projection of the Dimension Emperor".
He has two brothers, one of which exiled him in his childhood, knows maths and physics better than any human,
loves his mob family and soda.
His partner and bodyguard is Ains.
