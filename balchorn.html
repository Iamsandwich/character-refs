Balchorn

Balchorn is an old evil wizard who lives in a nice spookt castle from black stone, directly above an active volcano, which he supresses with his power. If something happens to him, the whole land all around him will perish in flames!

Balchorn is a human who lost his parents early on as a baby in a war against elves. The elven folk felt such guilt that they adopted him and raised him as good as they could. Balchorn learned how to live with nature and care for animals, but as a human, he coulnd't learn magic as fast as his peers. His hatred only grew when he realised how he came to be an orphan, and in his desperation he seeked forbidden books on summoning old gods and horrors to this realm.

He spent his days in his room, with a large old mirror, surrounded by books, scriptures and spells new and old. He learned how to speak to the old ones, and made a pact with a very powerful old diety, who is a star.

He became a powerful and feared wizard, destroyed his elven village, and conquered as much as he could before taking a break to build his nice castle.

Growing bored with his armies of orcs and demons, he embarked on some conquests to gain more land. However, in one of the villages he has found that the locals have already been badly chewed-up by some pale long-eard man instead.

They bickered and rivaled one another, until the conqueror reveraled he is the very god being Balchorn talked to in his younger years. He has gained a physical form in this realm, but only before his demise, and decided to visit him.

Overcome with grief, Balchorn devised a soul-bond spell to save him, and they now are planning to put it to use.

His beloved and deity is Elenran.
