Michael

Michael is a young cryptid hunter who grew up in an unfortunant family and decided to seek fame and riches by documenting and catching cryptids.

Imagine his suprise when he eventually finds out that he is one himself! Michael is a bird-aligned shadow creature. He finds that out after he catches Ophiuchus (Nakami) who is an old pink shark-aligned shadow himself.
Smart in many natural sciences and eventually finding a job as a mob doctor, he is the direct helper of Sammy, who he takes care of before Ains gets in the picture. He still checks on him daily, and is directly responsible for his wellbeing on Earth.

His soulmate and prized catch is Ophiuchus.
