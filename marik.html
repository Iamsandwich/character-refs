Marik

Marik is an evil wizard who studied under the school of the great Void and water wizard Morgan.

Marik was an able student, and ended up quickly learning all the needed spells before he left the school and set off to become a great wizard of his own. His main aligment is metal, and he wears it all over himself - his crown, chains on his clothes, heavy metal boots, he's all covered in it.

He even goes as far as to add metal to his body, eating it and storing it in his body as building material. It made his teeth change to a metal alloy, and his skull can be opened up like a machine to look at his brain.

He makes metal gollums from materials and human brains, and keeps cities under control with them. He uses the city mines to gather more metals for himself.

While Morgan is a kind wizard and doesn't support these shenanigains, he doesn't directly go to war with Marik, instead trying to persuade him to be better.

His beloved and teacher is Morgan.
