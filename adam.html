Adam

Adam is short for Adramalech. An old god long forgotten, with pockets full of gold is travelling the world in giref of his beloved Orcus, a god of death, after the big war.
Adam was no longer in charge as the sun, blood and wealth god, and in his grief found a new friend where he lost an old one. In the seawater where Orcus was drowned by Neptune, a new being emerged, an orca with half a human body and silvery hair. Adam named him Sterling, from sterling silver.

Not being able to swim, Adam seeked the merman's help disguising himself as a human, and travelling the world to find himself a place in this new life.

He eats gold, has plenty of it. Has a big peacock tail and feathers and donkey ears that he tries not to show off. Due to that most his forms feature longer ears. His skin is a gentle warm purple, his eyes pitch black with thin red rings on his sclera, and his bright burning red hair with a funny little point to the side make him a very colorful character.

He likes colors like gold, blue and green, and can do thread magic. Anything he can use as a thread, he can make into cloth and clothes of all kinds. He uses his own hair in extreme cases, as it is very sturdy. His hair shows his mood, with his tips becoming dark and then black when he's upset. He cries solid pearls, the pearls in the clams are the tears he spilled when he lost his dear Orcus.

Before he realised Orcus is yet again with him, he grieved by wearing fully covering clothes, and avoiding any food or drink.

In an alternate story Adam is a sun king of a large town, and he travels to kill a large dragon who terrifies the locals, ending up captured and living with the dragon on good terms, but not allowed to wander out of his territory.

His beloved is Orcus (Sterling).
