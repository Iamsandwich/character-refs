Love

Love was created as part of the Hylics rp universe.
Born all alone, and raised by ambulant skulls, she grew into a strange species that nobody seems to recognsie. Her sharp horns and dark eyes scared locals so much, she ended up living secluded on the moon.

She worked as an artist for the king of the moon for a while, until the royal knight named Fleam decided she should be free from it, and decided to invite her to live with him instead.

She dislikes dotted lines, and can tame ambulant skulls with no problem. She likes raw and cooked meat, and likes to bake sweet cakes.
Her unique ability is melting people to death by touching them with her hands, hence why she always wears special gloves. Her claws can be seen pokng through.

Her beloved is Fleam.
