Ames

Ames is a funny creature. He's a dragon-aligned shadow creature, with a long body, long snooty mask and bright red-gold scales and fur.

Ames has a human form with short black hair, a humanoid form with red fur and a human face, and a centaur form.

Think of him as a bright-red stoat or weasel! He loves eggs and his beloved Kyane (pronounced as Cyan or Kyane pepper). Ames is silly, speaks in a soft voice, and loves to talk about himself in third person. His eyes are bright yellow and big, he likes putting flowers in his hair.

He lives in the woods with his beloved old man, loves meat, EGGS and fruit.

He's playful and sweet, and very good at swimming and running about. His body is very long and he can make himself look very fuzzy.

His beloved is Kyane.
