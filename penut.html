Penut

Penut! A funny friend!

Penut (pronouned as Peanut) was created as part of the Eternal Cylinder universe.

Just like all the young treblum in the original story, Penut was hatched mere moments before having to adapt and run from the Eternal cylinder.

Penut is very friendly, sweet, likes his friends and is very emotional. One of the running jokes is an image of sad Penut with the captoion "Penut cried" that was born from an inside jokes from one of the Eternal Cylinder gameplay streams. The joke follows Penut's friends as they suffer through the dangers of their wolrd, with occasional casualties. No matter what, the joke ends with the caption "Penut cried".

Penut Thinks Volu, a yellow treblum with black spots, is the most handsome in their group. While Gwentue is the smartest.

Penut is a simple character that shines in his personality. He can be drawn in multiple different mutations, but his primary colors are deep gray and bright fuxcia.

He likes everyone, his best friends are Volu, Guseppe, Gwentue
