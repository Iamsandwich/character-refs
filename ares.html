Ares

Ares is based on the Satyriacs from "All tomorrows".
Living in a nice cozy apartment, he spent his days painting and really not doing much else. For his kind he isn't very social, and feeling like he doesn't belong, he volunteers to be a part of a new space colony on a different planet.

Artist are needed in a new world as much as engineers and doctors, and he gets accepted to be of the crew and a part of the new colony.
During the space travel an accident happens, Ares is not sure what happened, but he wakes up alone in a weird room that seems odd.
It later turns out everyone else on the ship died in a crash on a living planet, Anaphiel, the planet-person in question saved Ares. He now lives on the living planet, occasionally visiting his home planet with Anaphiel's help to show him his culture and visit his parents.

His beloved is Anaphiel.
