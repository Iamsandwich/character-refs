Bulin

Bulin is short for immunoglobulin! He is a guardian of a floating whale-like space organism. The inhabitants of the organism take on roles ofvarious cells, as the body creates them for building, repair, healing other cells, defending and seeking out food.

Bulin is a very important immune cell. He is made from old genes of the ship, and is one of the "direct links" - a cell that shares conciousness with the ship.

In a way, they are one person in different bodies. That makes Bulin's actions weighted and careful, he reacts fast and always knows what to do.

Rorygale, or Rory for short, was captured after he was attacking a rival ship. He was deemed a dangerous virus, collecting ship DNA to become stronger, but it seems like he had no interest in Bulin's ship.
He now has to guard his cell at all times, and use his powers for the good of the ship.

His partner and prisoner is Rorygale.
