Sylvio

Sylvio is a rich deap-sea diver, who owns a huge mansion near the sea and a lot of equipment for his diving expeditions.
He keeps large aquariums, labs, research stations, and knows enough himself to do analysis with them. He uncovers ancient and rare artifacts form the bottom of the sea, donates them to museums and keeps important information for himself.

He has a funny haircut with a "flower" in the middle, and bright green eyes. He's very good at swimming and holding his breath, and likes to have fun with his money.
He wears a special flashdrive as a necklace that lets him access important parts of his home, and various info he keeps.

He met Geoffrey in a bar while someone tried to hunt Sylvio down, and proved himself trustworthy. Geoffrey is not his real name, instead Sylvio insisted that he "looks like a Geoffrey".

In an alternate scenario he's a villian dictator, who owns an active volcano island. In that scenario Geoffrey's nickname is Mephistophiles, and he's a special spy from a hidden organisation who infiltrated Sylvio's base.

His beloved is Geoffrey.
