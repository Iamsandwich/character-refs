Olgard

Olgard is a deity who created the world.
Born from a big egg, he hatched as a big fuzzy caterpilliar, and grew into a large moth.
After creating by accident, and sometimes on purpose, many planets and stars, he made himself a nice planet to live on, where he keeps everything in check.
After various creatures started living all over the world, he watched them at a distance, bored and tired.
One day he noticed a particulary fun being who worshiped him, a young and strong elf named Rahiel who became a powerful warlord.
He blessed the funny man with all he'd like and after Rahiel passed away and was buried, invited him on his planet to live in harmony.

Olgard is a very cold and old god, the weight of the world pushes on him so hard, that ground cracks beneath him. His body and breath are very cold, but he can make it warm if he wishes.
He likes tasty food, nature and entertainment.
He enjoys watching life do its thing, but often gets tired of it and mostly rests with Rahiel.

He occasioanlly visits various places by disguising as a young man. It's easy to tell him apart by the patterns on his cheeks and how easily everything breaks under his weight.

In an alternate scenario, him and Rahiel enjoy pretending to be rivals in front of humans, and play out entire fihgts in front of them.

His beloved is Rahiel.
