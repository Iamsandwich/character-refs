Stone Prince

The Stone prince was inspired by the Grime universe. Made from sturdy polished black stone with golden veins in it, the prince is a ruler of his own land deep underground.

His kingdom got attacked and he was in the hiding before a stone knight made by the sheer will of the prince's people to protect him emerged and found him. The knight was named Snooty because of his funny helmet-like head.

The Stone prine is well-educated, polite and sophisticated. He is rather tall and very heavy, but he moves smoothly and doesn't crush things at all.

Traveling to a far kingdom for help, he had to work as a jester to talk to the king and ask him for help.

His soulmate and knight is Snooty.
