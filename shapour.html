Shapour

Shapour is a prince in a world full of sentient dragons. He has both a feral dragon form and a more human from, depending on what's needed in the story. But is usually portrayed more human-like.

He is a young prince in a land where his father rules with an iron fist. Taught everything a prince should know from a young age, he was never taught to be a proper member of society and grew rather antisocial. He has violent outbursts that could harm those around him and can be quite hot-headed. But in reality he is kind and does his best to not be like his father.
His advisor, Savred, taught him how to be wise and how to become a good ruler, together they will overthrow the king, which will be very easy as the people might do it themselves soon anyways.

His advisor and lover is Savred
