Damien

Damien is a character based on Alice in Wonderland. He's the March Hare and the brother of the White rabbit, one of many siblings in their big family.
He wears a fancy white suit, and has spots and patterns on his face, his hair is black with white streaks on them, and he has fuzzy white bunny ears with black spots!

Damien lives in a little house in the woods, he spends his day making toys and then selling them on the Wonderland market. He also fixes them for his clients and friends. He likes to rythme, be silly and jump about. He likes carrot cake and tea. And he has little red cups with white spots on them.

Damien meets the Hatter only after he comes out of jail, and mocks him, telling him silly stories and rythmes until they become friends.
Damien took Hatter into his house because he didn't have a place to live, together they adventure around the Wonderland. Frankly, they both dislike Alice for being a rude little girl.
In this version hatter is a goat-creature who is a part of personificaton of time. Being his silly side, he gets exiled from the main Time body and mind from time to time (heh). Hatter fears Time for his power and Time, sadly, dislikes Hatter a lot. Damien likes them both lots.

In an alternate scenario, Damien is much older and is a retired knight who keeps the funny goat man as a helper around his big mansion.

Another scenario portrays the wonderland as a secret organisation, where Hatter and Hare are important tactical agents who work out in the field and deal with anomalies. In that universe Damien's name is Dianne, and she's a young woman.

His beloved is Hatter (Time).
