Mozzy

Mozzy is a plant-alien from a far away planet, who actually was created as a character for among us. He is a silly blonde, very pale flower man, who is a prince of his planet.
His species can reproduce without the need of a partner, and that is exactly how Mozzy came to be. His father is ever so slightly different, because Mozzy still mutated in the process, but they do look near the same.
Wanting to explore the world, he left his home planet as a runaway prince, and stumbled onto a science spaceship, where he started causing trouble and met Arac.
He needs sunlight for his health, he loves sitting on warm soil, and his diet is carnivorous with traces of pure metals. Due to that he often eats wires and electrical appliences.
He blooms once a year from his hair, which is when he can choose to make offspring.
He wears a cheese wedge as decoration on his head sometimes, and has an alternate colorscheme with purple and white.
Can turn into a planty lizard creature with long sharp claws
Alternate universe - he is a big sea beast that lives in oceans of blood, rich with iron he needs so much.

His beloved is Arac.
