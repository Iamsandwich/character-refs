Ollie

Ollie, Olie, Oleander or Lucy, is a fallen angel Lucifer who got his halo lodged into his skull and forgot who he is. He since then lives thinking he's just a little weak imp named Oleander. On occasion his true identity still comes out, though.
Olgard, also going by the name of Sal, lived with him in heaven under the protection of Olgard, who exiled them both for being not very nice.

In an alternate universe Sal is just an old priest and Ollie spends his time pretending to be a young priest, haunting his house and pretending to be a white house cat.

His beloved is Sal (Raguel).
