Devious

Devious is a character set in the Hylics video game universe, but with his own twist.
Born as first of his kind from warm sands, he grew up near alone, being briefly taught language after a certain age, he was never properly socialised and grew up obessive and inclined to hurt people often.
He loves to cook, loves knives and blood, had all kinds of professions, from merchant to dancer, and royal musician. He is perfect for travel as his metabolism is minimal, and he can not feel temeprature at all. That makes it normal for him to sleep in water all night. He also sinks to the bottom so badly he can't even use sea ships.

Devious is a nickname used out of the universe, in the universe his name is Wayne, like any of his species. He is very anxious about being like the rest, and needs everyone to respect his name. He will not tolerate any nicknames.

In an alternate scenario he's a professional diver that keeps a pet merman.

His husband is Harvest.
His children and Barley, Cheeb and Chestnut.
