John

John, Jonny, Jon, or Joni was heavily based on the FNAF universe.
A lowly security guard, he spent his day sleeping in the vents, and working night shift in boredom.
Every night something weird would happen, but no lasting effects could ever alter Jon.
Jon is not his real name, it's a stolen name tag he collected from a different worker after he killed them. He keeps a collection of name tags an mementos. His actual name is Daniil, or Daniel. Spending most his time eating frozen hard pizza, without defrosting it, sewing up plushies and watching after the facility, he became isolated and bored.
His place of origin is Soviet Union, and his passport claims he's about 90.
He can't grow facial hair and his body seems to revert back to normal the next day after any incidents. As a sideffect he has a drawer full of his own teeth.
Eventually he met Frankie, who happened to be not a simple man himself, and together they figured out Jon is not a human, but instead a Shadow Creature with a lizard affinity.
He is still learning what that really is supposed to mean.

His beloved is Frankie. Main traits:
Loves marshmallows
Easily heals
Likes old music boxes
Lizard
Likes to swim
Sleeps all day
Likes knives and avoids firearms

Age:
Visually 21-23, in reality closer to 90
