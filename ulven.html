Ulven

Ulven is a young shadow creature. He is a Longclaw, a shadow being that lives in tightly connected packs with leader-based hierchy, featuring circles closest and furthest from the leader.
As a longclaw, Ulven can conver humans into partial shadows, as they become his personal obsessed slaves, he can use them for small simple jobs.
Ulven has a large pack that controls a big region, and has connection with the neighbouring city, for which he provides pelts, meat, protection and various jobs that are too hard or dangerous for humans.

As a leader of a big pack, he has a contract with local millitary forces, that dictate how humans and shadows should co-exist.

There are three scenarios which are equally valid for Ulven's character:

One being him still very young, before he accumulated a pack, and meeting Ceri, the cryptid hunter and photographer, and taking him in to protect him from the dangerous creature-filled woods.

The other, where he is an established pack member who meets Ceri, another Longclaw, with no pack, and helps him after a hunting injury, introducing him into the pack.

The third scenario is Ulven being an established pack leader who is sold a human by the millitary, as a potential new material to become a slave and worker. However, in this case Ceri is infected by a skinwalker, and seems to be altered even before any changes are made to him by Ulven. This leads to Ceri of that universe to be particulary obidient, as a soldier, but also unstable and strong due to the infection which layers onto the shadow creature genes.

His feral form looks like a tall balck and white maned wolf with a very long nose and big ears. Some people mistake him for a strange breed of borzoi.

His beloved is Ceri.
