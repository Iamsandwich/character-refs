Clavious

Clavious is based in the Hylics universe, and is by his race a rancher. Blue skin and soft horn-like growths in his head are the main visual traits, but he also is born in a small tribe where he was supposed to gather food and watch over juice beasts.

Not being a very good rancher, he became a good thief instead. Lonely and near exiled, he lived alone in his hut and drank his evening away until he met Alachor.
The fancy wizard took him under his wing and taught him magic and various sciences. And now he lives in a fancy mantion, learning magical literature and nice spells while getting kissed.

His partner and teacher is Alachor.

Main traits:
Blue
Soft tentacle-like "horns"
A little cleptomanic
Good hunter
Knows light magic


Age:
About 21-23
