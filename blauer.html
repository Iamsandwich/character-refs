Blauer

Blauer was initialy made as a dragon character. With long webbed ears and a bright-blue body, he was initially a flight rising mirror dragon breed, who was very solitary and thus became obsessed with mirrors that replaced other dragons for him.

An able healer, he lived in his tower and made bug snacks and medicinal salves, occasioanlly helping those who'd come ill to him.

One day a fae dragon stumbled into his home ill, and he had to nurture him back to health.

He was given alternate forms like an old wizard secluded in a tower, forced to teach a young man from a royal family.

In another version he is a servant of a rich man who is a jeweler and a druid in secret, as well as a big sand worm.

In yet another iteration he's a younger man in the 19th century who buys a nice mantion only to figure out it's posessed by a lonely ghost.

In all the versions Blauer is a son of owner of a winery, although in some of them, less successfull. His blue hair color is said to bring luck to his family.

He is an able magician who is nature-aligned, doesn't like crowds, and in his dragon variation rather good at flying. So much so, that he prefers tall hard-to-get-to towers.

While in his original dragon design he has big webbed wings, in his human design he has blue or gray butterfly wings.

His partner (Student/Master/Ghostlty man) is Avel.
